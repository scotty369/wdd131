Workout Splits

Full Body

A great option for beginners or those with limited workout days. Each session targets multiple muscle groups.

  • Example exercises: Squats, Bench Press, Deadlifts, Pull-Ups

Upper/Lower Split

Alternate between upper body and lower body workouts. Rest, then repeat. Ideal for intermediate lifters.

  • Example Upper Body: Pull-Ups, Bench Press, Overhead Press
  • Example Lower Body: Squats, Lunges, Romanian Deadlifts

Push, Pull, Legs

Three-day rotation focusing on specific muscle groups. Perfect for building strength and hypertrophy.

  • Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps): Bench Press, Shoulder Press
  • Pull (Back, Biceps): Deadlifts, Pull-Ups
  • Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes): Squats, Bulgarian Split Squats
